Suburra
—The Game

— Art direction & launch teaser

We had the opportunity to work with Monogrid and Think Cattleya for their new project Suburra the game, based on the popular Italian Netflix series.

We directed all the concept art of the videogame and made the launch teaser of the app, available on every app store.

Taking inspiration from the low poly design from the 80s and 90s games, we created flat backgrounds where the three protagonists, Aureliano, Spadino and Lele, move between the worlds of politics, organised crime and the obscure side of Vatican. 

A dark atmosphere permeates also the teaser, where glimpses of bloody red highlights the crucial moments of the story, making Rome not such a sunny city.
